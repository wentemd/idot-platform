Frequently Asked Questions

What data is included in the platform? +
Our database contains over 1.18 million bid records from Illinois Department of Transportation (IDOT) bid tabulations spanning 2018-2025. This includes data from 6,900+ contracts, 500+ contractors, and 12,400+ unique pay items across all 114 Illinois counties.
How does the 7-day free trial work? +
Start your Pro subscription with full access to all features. You won't be charged until your 7-day trial ends. Cancel anytime during the trial and you won't be billed. No commitment required.
What is the Estimator Tool? +
The Estimator Tool lets you upload an Excel file containing pay items and quantities. It automatically returns weighted average unit prices based on historical winning bids, filtered by your selected districts and date range. Perfect for quickly pricing out entire bid sheets.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. For monthly plans, you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. For annual plans, you'll have access for the remainder of your subscription year.
How accurate is the pricing data? +
Our data is parsed directly from official IDOT bid tabulation documents. While we strive for accuracy, the data may contain parsing errors or be incomplete. We recommend using this data as a reference point and always verifying critical pricing decisions with official IDOT sources.
Do you offer refunds? +
We offer a 7-day free trial so you can evaluate the platform before committing. If you're unsatisfied after subscribing, contact us within 14 days of your first payment for a full refund. After 14 days, we do not offer refunds but you can cancel to prevent future charges.
